ROVIAL

Space Robotics

As the space industry expands, the demand for innovative and sustainable tools is also on the rise. Therefore, Rovial Space company is working on an ambitious project that aims to use a robotics system for building and repairing space structures.

As a robotics engineer, my work during this project was centered around these main objectives:

  • mechatronics design of a robotic system capable of performing assembly and repair tasks;
  • create various controllers to test and validate the robotic system during the experimental proof-of-concept.
